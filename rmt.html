Restorative Massage Therapy Facials Webpage Redesign

Timeline

1 Month

Tools

The Project


As a part of my internship as a Creative Team member at Bluestone Communications—a student ran Public Relations firm—I had the opportunity to design and create a webpage for Restorative Massage Therapy. The task was to create a thorough webpage about the facial services Restorative Massage Therapy offers to their customers. Not only did I have to come up with a design that made sense for their already existing website, but I had the opportunity to collaborate with other Creative Team members to conduct SEO research and implementation on the webpage as well.


Planning


This was the first opportunity I had to work with actual clients and provide exceptional work regarding their products. I started off this project by meeting with my Account Executive to discuss the wants and needs of the client so that I could ensure I delivered work that they would find valuable.


After the initial meeting, I met with other members of the Bluestone Creative Team to go over content and SEO ideas for the Facials page. This was a great opportunity to collaborate with other team members to discuss and guarantee that the project requirements were being met.


Design


Once we had the content and SEO material ready to go, I was able to come up with the design for the webpage and present it to the owner of Restorative Massage Therapy. I was excited to be able to present my design choices and decisions with an actual stakeholder and receive feedback on what went well and what needed to be revised.




As I have been promoted to a Creative Team Associate at Bluestone Communications for the Spring 2021 semester, this is an ongoing project for the upcoming semester where I will work on providing other updates to the Restorative Massage Therapy website with other members of the Creative Team.
